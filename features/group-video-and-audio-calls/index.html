Fast, Ad-hoc Group Discussions

Just click on a team member to start an audio call. Once you're in a call, click on any other user to quickly bring them into your call.

Simultaneous Sharing

Easily share your screen and broadcast video at the same time. In a group call, others can even share their screen at the same time you do!

Ultra High Quality

Our video and audio are extremely high quality, even with multiple participants in the same call! Echo cancelation is also top notch, making group calls much more pleasant.

See & Join Ongoing Calls

With Speak, you can see other ongoing calls as they are happening. When you're ready to join, just click the call and you're instantly brought in.
