Speak is a great alternative to Webex. Here's why you should consider making the switch:

1) Speak is integrated into your desktop

Unlike Webex, Speak is built directly into your desktop. The optional people dock lives on your screen and gives you a window into your coworkers.

Speak's presence detection is also extremely granular. When you see a green dot next to someone, this means they are actually sat at their computer and available. If you'd like to talk with them, just click their picture. It's that easy.

2) Speak is easier to use

Since Speak is built into your desktop, talking with your team doesn't get any easier. Simply click someone's face to start a call with them. If you'd like to add others to the call, click there face and they'll be brought in. No more fiddling with meeting links, or constantly asking "hey, are you there?". Speak is like a communication super power for your team.

The UX is awesome. Really simple way of starting a video chat with a coworker. I love the dock and user presence based on activity.

3) We're a smaller, quicker team

Our goal is to provide the best communication tool possible. We can only do this if every team member has a deep understanding of our customers inside and out. So when you send us a support email, it goes directly into a shared inbox that the entire team reads and responds to. Responses take no less than 12 hours and most are within minutes!

Being a smaller team also makes us more nimble. We make daily improvements to the product and are constantly squashing bugs and adding new features. We even have a public roadmap so you can stay on top of what changes are coming. We also discuss ongoing features in our public issue tracker. Talk about transparency!

Ready to try out Speak?

Switching to Speak couldn't be easier. Simply download the app, invite your teammates, and you're off to the races!

In no time at all we're confident you'll see the huge benefits of using Speak for your team communication.
