Speak is integrated into your desktop

Unlike Webex, Speak is built direclty into your desktop, but stays out of your way until you need it. This means that your team mates are always a click away. And with Speak's accurate presence detection, you always know who's available and ready to talk.

Speak is easier to use

With Speak, it's as simple as clicking on your team mate's face to start an instant call. No more fiddling with meeting links, or constantly asking "hey, are you there?". Speak makes daily team communication simpler than ever!

"The UX is awesome. Really simple way of starting a video chat with a co-worker. I love the dock and user presence based on activity."

We're a smaller, quicker team

When you send a support email to Speak, it goes directly into a shared inbox that the entire team reads and responds to. This allows everyone on the team to keep in touch with our customers.

Being a smaller team also makes us more nimble. We make daily improvements to the product and are constantly squashing bugs and adding new features. We even have a public roadmap so you can stay on top of what changes are coming.

Ready to try out Speak?

Switching to Speak couldn't be easier. Simply download the app, invite your teammates, and you're off to the races!

In no time at all we're confident you'll see the huge benefits of using Speak for your team communication.
