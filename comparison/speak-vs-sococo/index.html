Speak is a great alternative to Sococo. Here's why you should consider making the switch:

1) Speak is easier to use

With Speak, jumping into a conversation is as simple as clicking on your coworker's face. Once you're in a call, Speak stays out of your way so you're free to use your computer, but still know what's going on in your call.

2) Speak is much lighter

Unlike Sococo, Speak doesn't try take up your whole screen and bog you down with a bulky interface. Instead, it's integrated into your desktop so it's super light and works well with all of your other applications, especially your team chat tool!

The interface and UX is beautiful. We'll definitely keep using it. You guys are on to something big here :-)

4) Speak plays well with your team chat tool

Speak works alongside your team chat tool to provide a seamless experience. And if you're using Slack, you can start a call just by typing "/speak name". Does it get much easier?

Ready to try out Speak?

Switching to Speak couldn't be simpler. Simply download the app, invite your teammates, and you're off to the races!

In no time at all we're confident you'll see the huge benefits of using Speak for your team communication.
