Speak is built for quick team communication

If you're looking for quick, daily team 1-on-1 and group communication, GoToMeeting just doesn't cut it. Speak, on the other hand, was built from the ground up to make daily communication a breeze for large and small teams alike.

Our interface is designed to stay open all day, without getting in your way. This means that your team is always at your finger tips. Speak even knows if they've stepped away from their computer!

Speak is lighter

GoToMeeting certainly has a lot of bells and whistles, but when you just want to have your meeting and get on with your day, these "features" just get in the way and make things confusing.

Conversely, Speak was designed to be a pleasant and intuitive experience. It gives you all the features you need in order to stay in communication with your team, without all the bloat.

"Speak is awesome!!! I am currently trying to get my entire team using it, easily the best communication tool I've used in the recent years!"

We provide a much better customer experience

Each team member participates in customer support via a shared support inbox. And since our team spans multiple timzones, this means that most support requests are handled within an hour, and most within a matter of minutes!

Being a smaller team also makes us more nimble. We make daily improvements to the product and are constantly squashing bugs and adding new features. We even have a public roadmap so you can stay on top of what changes are coming.

Ready to try out Speak?

Switching to Speak couldn't be easier. Simply download the app, invite your teammates, and you're off to the races!

In no time at all we're confident you'll see the huge benefits of using Speak for your team communication.
