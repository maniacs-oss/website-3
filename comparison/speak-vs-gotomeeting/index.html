If you're looking for quick, daily team 1-on-1 and group communication, GoToMeeting just doesn't cut it. Speak, on the other hand, was built from the ground up to make daily communication a breeze for large and small teams alike.

Our interface is designed to stay open all day, without getting in your way. This means that your team is always at your finger tips. Speak even knows if they've stepped away from their computer!

Speak is lighter

Speak was designed to be a pleasant and intuitive experience. It gives you all the features you need in order to stay in communication with your team, without all the bloat.

I look forward to using your product and it is a welcome relief from the madness of gotomeeting, webex, uberconference, etc.
