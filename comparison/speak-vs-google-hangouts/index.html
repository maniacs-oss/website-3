Speak is a great alternative to Google Hangouts. Here's why you should consider making the switch:

Speak is faster and easier

Unlike Hangouts, Speak is extremely simple and lightweight, so you don't have to waste time fiddling with the software. It contains everything you need to have a quick, productive call, and get on with your day.

Starting a Hangout can also be quite complicated. Messing around with links and Google accounts can get pretty confusing! And the problems only grow worse the more people you add.

Speak, on the other hand, is integrated directly into your desktop, and starting a call is as simple as clicking your coworker's face!

Speak works much better than Google Hangouts and all other products we've used. You guys did a good job on it, and we expect to keep using it!

Speak is built for teams

Hangouts is designed for scheduled meetings. It works best when you setup a time in the future and invite everyone over email in advance. Speak works well for scheduled meetings but also lets you have quick, ad-hoc discussions throughout the day.

You can also see other meetings as they are happening and choose to join with one click. This is particularly great for tight knit, collaborative teams.

Speak offers support from real people
