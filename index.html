Built for teams

We've designed Speak to let your team work better together. Choose to make yourself instantly available without calling. You can even see other calls that are happening in real time.

Super fast & reliable

You don’t want to fight with your tools, they should just work and get out of the way. With Speak, it’s as simple as clicking on a team mate to join a call that connects in under a second.

Know who's available

Speak's presence is automatic and incredibly accurate, so you always know whether your team mates are there or not. You can also add custom status messages to provide more context.
