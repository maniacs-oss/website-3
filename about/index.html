Our Mission

Team communication is hard. So much time is wasted fiddling with tools, sharing meeting links and setting up calls.

Well we say no more!

We believe that your meeting tool should be there when you need it, then get the heck out of your way! With Speak, teams can say what they need to say, then get on with their day.

Meet the Team

We're a team of 4 working from all around the world. We're crafting the perfect communication tool while working from coffee shops, co-working spaces and home offices so that your team can too!
