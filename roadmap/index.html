--- layout: page title: Roadmap description: Here's what we're planning for future versions of the Speak app and platform. Don't see your idea listed here? Get in contact, we'd love to hear about it! ---

  1. Sept
    2015

    Windows

    This has been our most requested feature for quite a while. At the beginning of September we began sending out invites for the beta version of our Windows client. So far feedback has been great and bugs have been minimal, we're fixing everything that comes in and expect to have a full Windows release available by the end of the month!

  2. Sept
    2015

    Pricing

    We plan on soft launching pricing before the end of September, this will let those that are getting value out of Speak start paying and helping support continued development. We'll also be offering an unlimited trial for teams that are unable to pay or need an extended period of time to evaluate the platform.

  3. October
    2015

    Web Access

    The idea here is to have a web accessible version of Speak that can be used by guests and those outside of your organization to join into calls without having to download the full app. Of course, the web client will be seamlessly integrated with the desktop version of Speak.

  4. October
    2015

    Meeting Scheduling

    There are two types of conversations in the business world, those that are ad-hoc and spontaneous (Speak is great at those!), and those calls that are scheduled ahead of time such as a weekly meeting. We plan to make this second type of conversation super easy on the Speak platform.

  5. November
    2015

    Open API

    Behind the scenes we've been working on a RESTful API to access Speak's core functionality such as starting and stopping calls, getting who's online and other interesting bits. We'd love to open this up to developers and integrations before the end of the year.

  6. November
    2015

    Integrations

    Using our open API to build deeper, easily installable integrations with your existing tools, such as Slack, Hipchat, Google Calendar and many more services.
